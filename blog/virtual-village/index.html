Virtual Village

Concept

Designed for Xplore Athens as a digital update of the classic play mat, the concept was to allow players to create their own play space with interactive roads, railways and rivers.

Gameplay

Players are presented with a top down view of an island, rear projected onto a large glass surface. They also have a number of physical road, rail, river and air tokens which they can place on the surface.

Each token detected becomes a node in it's specific path and when at least 2 nodes have been placed, traffic will start to flow along the path.

A second wall mounted screen flips between a zoomed out view of the island and views from vehicles moving along the various paths.

Nodes will also spawn buildings in the vicinity, the more nodes the more buildings will spawn.

Technology

Hardware

  • custom build table with glass rear projection surface and overhead camera
  • Windows PC
  • Second display to show views round the island

Software

  • Main game developed in Unity
  • Custom C++ app using openCV to detect player tokens and send positions to Unity app

Development

Developed in Unity and C++, this was a solo development project with 3d models provided by an artist on my team. The rivers, roads and railways are entirely procedurally generates.
