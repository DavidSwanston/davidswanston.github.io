Seasonal Projections

Concept

Developed for a new, purpose built seasonal event space at Sundown Adventureland. These animations are projected on a 9m x 3m wall and blend into a permanent painted mural in the space to help theme the area.

Snowman loses his hat in a snowball fight

The animations run on a 4 minute loop with constant subtle movement. Throughout the animations, various moments occur (the snowmen have a snowball fight, a snowflake lands on the nose of one of the trees, who then sneezes, an elf zips by on a sledge).

Spring / Easter projection

Development

All modelling, rigging and animation for the winter scene was done by me in Blender and rendered in Eevee. Many of the features in both animations allowed me to get to grips with the new geometry nodes pipeline.

The Humpty Dumpty model and tractor models in the spring scene were created by another artist on our team who took over to do the Autumn and Winter animations.
