Truck Escape

Overview

  • Developed in Unity
  • 2 person development team
  • Off axis projection
  • Path Following
  • Scripted Events
  • Vehicle Physics
  • Tiled Terrain

Developed as part of the Tomb Raider LIVE experience in London. Players find themselves in the back of a themed truck, being chased by mercenaries in vehicles. They have to blast the enemies with crossbows to escape.
