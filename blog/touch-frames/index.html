Prototypes

Overview

A collection of mini-game prototypes I built as proof of concept for the physical object detection IR frame hardware we were building. These were designed to be played with standard NERF blasters.

Smash the cups

  • Players fire darts at the cups to smash them.
  • Hitting the recycle button below the cup causes the smashed parts to reform back into a new cup.

Ropes

  • Hitting a cup or ball will flip it's colour using a shader effect
  • Ropes have dynamic physics, allowing for tangles

Ducks and Boxes

  • Ducks and boxes fall from the top left onto the conveyor belts
  • Conveyors start with random speed and direction
  • Hitting an arrow on a conveyor will move the speed gauge one step in that direction
  • Hitting the speed gauge on a conveyor will set it to a random speed and direction
  • Players aim is to get as many boxes into the end goal and as few ducks as possible
